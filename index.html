Maintenance history

Four years of documented maintenance and upgrades

The record below covers the present ownership, including professional yard work, equipment upgrades, winter storage and annual underwater-hull maintenance.

2026

Rigging, energy system and structural improvements

  • Rigging: new upper shrouds and backstay, complete with new turnbuckles; new shroud retainers at the spreader tips; new turnbuckle covers.
  • Engine and drivetrain: raw-water pump impeller replaced; new shaft seal installed; full engine service carried out by Davids Werft.
  • Solar and electrical: two new 100 W solar panels and a new 12 V automotive socket in the forecabin.
  • Bow and anchoring: new chain pipe into the forepeak for the anchor rode, with a custom marine-plywood box inside the forepeak; new bow platform.
  • Cockpit and deck: removable cockpit sole section added for access to the propeller shaft; stainless-steel hatch hinges replaced.
  • Exterior woodwork: all exterior wooden components reinforced with epoxy and finished with three coats of Epifanes varnish; the tiller received five coats.
  • Underwater hull: old antifouling sanded and renewed with two coats of Hempel Mille NCT; propeller coated with Hempel Silic One.
  • Interior: new mirror installed.
2025

Major hull, deck and sail-handling refit

  • Running rigging: new main halyard and dedicated mast block; new genoa sheets.
  • Hull finish: hull repainted in her distinctive yellow colour using Epifanes Mono-Urethane.
  • Deck finish: deck repainted white using Epifanes Mono-Urethane and a new KiwiGrip non-slip coating applied.
  • Engine: full engine service carried out by Davids Werft.
  • Plumbing: new mixer tap and hand-held bidet spray installed.
  • Winter storage: yacht stored indoors in a hangar.
  • Underwater hull: old antifouling sanded and renewed with two coats of Hempel Mille NCT; propeller coated with Hempel Silic One.
2024

Navigation, deck equipment and keel maintenance

  • Engine and cooling: full engine service carried out by Davids Werft; fuel pre-filter and raw-water strainer installed.
  • Navigation instruments: new Silva 100 compass, Windex and NASA Marine Clipper Wind, Depth and Log instruments.
  • Visibility and lighting: new radar reflectors and LED lighting installed inside and outside.
  • Deck hardware: winches stripped, serviced and lubricated; Barton Winchers fitted; new rail-mounted anchor holder installed.
  • Sails and canvas: genoa seams renewed; sprayhood clear window and seams renewed.
  • Sealing: locker and hatch seals renewed.
  • Keel: keel bolts and the keel-to-hull joint professionally serviced by Davids Werft.
  • Winter storage and underwater hull: yacht stored indoors in a hangar; propeller coated with Hempel Silic One.
2023

Purchase and electrical-system upgrade

  • Rassvet purchased by the current owner.
  • Charging: Victron Energy 15 A MPPT solar charge controller and Victron 230 V / 30 A shore-power charger installed.
  • Electrical protection: new 16 A circuit breakers installed.
  • Batteries: two new 105 Ah AGM batteries installed.
  • Bilge system: new bilge pump installed.
  • Underwater hull: the previous owner renewed the epoxy barrier coating before the sale.
